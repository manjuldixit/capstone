## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q: What documents do I need to apply for equipment financing?**  
A: Essential documents include business registration, 2 years ITR, 6 months bank statements, equipment quotation, and personal ID proof. For companies, board resolution is also needed.

**Q: How long does the approval process take?**  
A: Standard process takes 10-15 days from submission. Express processing can complete in 72 hours if documents are complete.

**Q: Can I get financing for used equipment?**  
A: Yes, we finance both new and used equipment. Used equipment is financed based on condition, residual value, and valuation report.

**Q: What is the maximum tenure available?**  
A: Equipment term loans can be financed for up to 15 years (180 months), depending on equipment type and business profile.

**Q: Is insurance mandatory?**  
A: Yes, comprehensive equipment insurance is mandatory and costs 0.8% p.a., which can be included in the EMI.

**Q: Can I prepay my equipment loan?**  
A: Yes, prepayment is allowed after 6 months with a 2% prepayment penalty.

**Q: What if I need additional funds after getting a loan?**  
A: You can apply for Equipment Line of Credit against existing equipment, or refinance existing loan for additional funds.

**Q: How do dealers apply for the Vendor Financing Program?**  
A: Contact our Business Development team or visit our vendor portal for registration and program details.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which product should I choose for different needs?**
A: Personal loans for immediate personal needs, business loans for business expansion, credit cards for convenience spending, savings accounts for emergency funds, fixed deposits for long-term wealth.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: For loans, penalties apply and credit score suffers. For credit cards, late fees and higher interest. For deposits, no risk - funds fully insured.

**Q: Are my deposits safe?**
A: Yes, all deposits covered by DICGC insurance up to ₹10,00,000, making them completely risk-free.

**Q: Can I get loans against deposits?**
A: Yes, FDs can be used as collateral for personal loans at preferential rates, or directly borrow against FD at 2% above FD rate.

**Q: What's the difference between simple and compound interest?**
A: Simple = fixed interest on principal. Compound = interest earned on principal + accumulated interest (higher returns over time).
