# Loan Guidelines & Eligibility

*Comprehensive Guidelines for Personal and Business Loans*

---

## Personal Loan Eligibility

### Core Requirements

**Age & Citizenship:**
- Indian citizen or OCI (Overseas Citizen of India)
- Age: 21-65 years (minimum 21, maximum 65 at maturity)
- Age at loan maturity should not exceed 70 years (for some products)

**Employment Status:**
- Salaried employees: Permanent or contract (min 6 months tenure)
- Self-employed: Minimum 2 years in business
- Proprietors: Minimum 3 years in business
- Freelancers: Minimum 2 years consistent income
- Not acceptable: Unemployed, homemakers without income

**Income Requirements:**
- Minimum annual income: ₹30,000 (₹2,500/month)
- Better approval: ₹50,000+ annual income
- Income stability: Consistent for minimum 6-12 months
- Primary + secondary income: Both considered
- Spouse income: Can be considered for joint applications

**Credit Profile:**
- Minimum credit score: 650 (standard approval)
- Preferred credit score: 700+ (better terms)
- Credit score 800+: Premium rates
- No default: Must not have defaulted in last 24 months
- No ongoing defaults: All previous debts must be current
- Bounce checks: Maximum 2 in last 2 years acceptable

**Residency:**
- Indian resident for minimum 1 year
- Proof of residence: Utility bill, Aadhar, rental agreement
- NRI policies: Separate guidelines apply

### Employment Type Specific Criteria

**For Salaried Individuals:**
- Permanent employee status preferred (contract acceptable with 6+ months tenure)
- Minimum monthly salary: ₹2,500
- Salary credited directly to bank account
- Industry: Private, public, government, PSU sectors acceptable
- Job stability: Minimum 6 months in current position
- Age at retirement: Should not exceed 65 years

**For Self-Employed/Proprietors:**
- Business operation: Minimum 2-3 years
- Monthly income: ₹3,000+ minimum
- Audited financials: Mandatory for loans >₹5L
- Business registration: Valid business license/PAN
- Profit margins: Consistent or growing
- Market reputation: Good standing with suppliers/customers

**For Freelancers:**
- Consistent income: 2+ years of regular payments
- Client references: 3+ ongoing clients preferred
- Income documentation: Bank statements + contracts
- Projects: Ongoing/repeat projects preferred over one-time

### Income Verification Methods

**For Salaried:**
1. **Primary Documents:**
   - Last 3 months salary slips (consecutive, unbroken)
   - Offer letter and appointment letter
   - Employee ID proof
   - Last 2 years Income Tax Returns (ITR)
   
2. **Verification Process:**
   - Bank statement analysis (6-12 months)
   - Salary credit pattern verification
   - Allowances breakdown analysis
   - EPF/contribution verification (if applicable)
   - Employer contact verification (in select cases)

**For Self-Employed:**
1. **Primary Documents:**
   - Last 2 years audited financial statements
   - Last 2 years Income Tax Returns
   - Profit & Loss statement
   - Balance sheet
   - Business registration documents
   
2. **Verification Process:**
   - GST return analysis (if registered)
   - TDS payment verification
   - Business bank statement analysis (12-24 months)
   - Profit trend analysis (consistency/growth)
   - Collateral/asset verification

**For Business Owners:**
1. **Additional Documents:**
   - Partnership deed (if applicable)
   - Auditor's report
   - CIF (Client Information Form)
   - Turnover declaration
   
2. **Verification Process:**
   - Collateral valuation
   - Creditor/debtor verification
   - Market reputation check
   - Industry analysis

### Credit Score Impact & Interest Rate Slabs

**Credit Score Based Pricing:**
| Credit Score | Interest Rate | Processing Fee | Approval Probability |
|--------------|--------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| 650-675 | 12% p.a. | 2% | 65-70% |
| 675-700 | 11.5% p.a. | 1.75% | 75-80% |
| 700-750 | 10.5% p.a. | 1.5% | 85-90% |
| 750-800 | 9.5% p.a. | 1.25% | 92-95% |
| 800+ | 8.5% p.a. | 1% | 95%+ |

**Factors Affecting Rate (Beyond Credit Score):**
- Income stability: ±0.5%
- Loan amount: Higher amounts may get better rates
- Tenure: Longer tenure slightly higher rate
- Employment tenure: Government sector lower rates
- Existing relationship: Existing customers get discounts
- Floating vs fixed: Fixed rates 0.5% higher

**Credit History Considerations:**
- Recent missed payments: Rate increase 1-2%
- Multiple credit inquiries: Rate increase 0.5%
- High credit utilization: Rate increase 0.5-1%
- Settled loans: Positive factor, rate decrease 0.5%
- Perfect payment history: Rate decrease 0.5-1%

### Additional Eligibility Factors

**Co-applicant/Guarantor:**
- Can be spouse, parent, sibling, or friend
- Must be co-breadwinner or guarantor
- Co-applicant's income added to application
- Guarantor provides personal guarantee only
- Both liable for repayment

**Existing Liabilities:**
- Credit card outstanding: Should be <30% of credit limit
- Other loans: Total EMI should be <50% of monthly income
- Overdraft/LOC: Should be in good standing
- Home loan: Considered in debt-to-income ratio

**Property/Collateral:**
- Unsecured: Up to ₹3-5L based on profile
- Secured: Against property, FD, securities
- Personal guarantee: On all loans
- Collateral margin: 20-30% for property-backed loans

## Business Loan Eligibility

### Core Requirements

**Business Profile:**
- Business operating: Minimum 2 years (3 years preferred)
- Business structure: Sole proprietorship, partnership, company, LLP, trust
- Legal status: Properly registered with all compliances
- Sector: Most sectors covered (restricted sectors need approval)
- Annual turnover: Minimum ₹5,00,000

**Owner Profile:**
- Age: 25-65 years (at loan maturity: not exceeding 70)
- Citizenship: Indian citizen or OCI
- Experience: Minimum 2-3 years in business
- Track record: Clean repayment history
- Industry knowledge: Adequate understanding of business

**Financial Profile:**
- Annual revenue: Minimum ₹5,00,000 (₹10L+ preferred)
- Profitability: Net profit margin minimum 5-10%
- Growth trajectory: Stable or growing business
- Cash flow: Positive and consistent
- Debt capacity: Sufficient to service proposed loan

**Credit Profile:**
- Owner credit score: Minimum 700
- Business credit history: Clean, no defaults
- Loan repayment: All past loans repaid on time
- Defaults: Zero defaults in last 3 years
- Credit inquiries: No excessive inquiries

### Business Type Specific Requirements

**Manufacturing Sector:**
- Land & building ownership preferred
- Machinery: Modern, well-maintained
- Capacity utilization: Minimum 60%
- Supply chain: Established, reliable
- Product demand: Growing or stable
- Environmental clearances: All required approvals

**Retail & Trading:**
- Inventory management: Efficient turnover
- Customer base: Diversified, repeat customers
- Premises: Owned or long-term lease (3+ years)
- Working capital: Regular requirement
- Seasonal factors: Considered in planning

**Services Sector:**
- Client contracts: Long-term, stable
- Revenue consistency: Predictable, recurring
- Skilled workforce: Trained, experienced
- Competitive advantage: Clear differentiation
- Growth potential: Expandable model

**IT & Professional Services:**
- Revenue model: Project-based or retainer
- Client retention: High (80%+ recurring)
- Team strength: Qualified professionals
- Market position: Established reputation
- Export revenue: Plus factor

**Agriculture & Agro-business:**
- Land ownership: Clear titles
- Crop/product type: Economically viable
- Irrigation: Facilities in place
- Experience: 3+ years in agriculture
- Market linkage: Established sales channel

### Document Requirements

**Business Registration & Ownership:**
- Certificate of Incorporation / Registration (GoM/ROC)
- Partnership deed (with amendments)
- Memorandum & Articles of Association (for companies)
- Personal PAN and business PAN
- GST registration certificate
- Business address proof (utility bill, lease deed)
- Trade license / industry license
- Ownership deed (if premises owned)

**Financial Documents (Last 2-3 Years):**
- Income Tax Returns (audited for companies)
- Profit & Loss statement
- Balance sheet
- Bank statements (business account, 12-24 months)
- GST returns (quarterly/monthly, last 4-8 returns)
- TDS payments evidence
- Accounts receivable aging
- Accounts payable aging

**Project/Proposal Documents:**
- Business plan for proposed project
- Detailed project report (for large loans)
- Cost estimation/quotations
- Revenue projections
- Breakeven analysis
- Market research/feasibility study
- Use of funds breakdown

**Collateral Documents:**
- Property ownership documents
- Recent property valuation report
- Property tax receipts
- Encumbrance certificate
- Insurance policy copies
- Stock/inventory valuation
- Machinery schedule

**Compliance Documents:**
- Board resolution (for companies)
- Power of attorney (if applicable)
- Auditor's certificate
- Statutory compliance certificates
- Environmental/pollution clearance (if required)
- Factory license (if applicable)

### Loan Amount Determination Framework

**Turnover Based Approval:**
| Annual Turnover | Loan Amount (Multiple) | Maximum Exposure |
|-----------------|----------------------|------------------|
| ₹5-10L | 1-1.5x | ₹15L |
| ₹10-50L | 2-2.5x | ₹1.25Cr |
| ₹50L-1Cr | 2.5-3x | ₹3Cr |
| ₹1-5Cr | 2-3x | ₹15Cr |
| ₹5Cr+ | 2-3x | Case-by-case |

**Profitability Adjustments:**
- Net profit margin <5%: Reduced approval
- Net profit margin 5-10%: Standard approval
- Net profit margin 10-15%: Enhanced approval
- Net profit margin >15%: Premium terms

**Collateral Coverage Required:**
- Unsecured: Up to ₹10-25L (based on profile)
- Secured against property: 75-80% LTV (Loan-to-Value)
- Secured against FDs/securities: 90% LTV
- Personal guarantee: Mandatory on all loans

**Debt Service Capacity:**
- Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) minimum: 1.25x
- DSCR = (Net profit + depreciation) / (EMI + other debt service)
- Lower DSCR: Reduced loan amount or shorter tenure
- Higher DSCR: Favorable terms

### Interest Rate Structure

**Base Rate (as on April 2026):**
- Base lending rate: 7.5-8% p.a.

**Spread Based on Sector & Risk:**
| Sector | Risk Category | Spread | Total Rate |
|--------|--------------|--------|-----------|
| Manufacturing | Medium | 2-3% | 9.5-11% |
| Retail | Medium-High | 2.5-3.5% | 10-11.5% |
| Services | Low | 1.5-2% | 9-10% |
| IT/Export | Low | 1-2% | 8.5-10% |
| Agriculture | High | 3-4% | 10.5-12% |

**Adjustments to Base Rate:**
- Loan amount <₹10L: +0.5%
- Loan amount >₹50L: -0.5%
- Tenure >60 months: +0.25%
- Existing customer: -0.25%
- Strong financial profile: -0.5%

**Floating vs Fixed Rate:**
- Floating: Base + spread (monthly reset)
- Fixed: Fixed rate for loan tenure
- Fixed premium: +0.5% over floating rate

## Loan Processing & Approval

### Processing Steps & Timelines

**Stage 1: Application & Preliminary Check (1 day)**
- Application submission and document collection
- Completeness check of documents
- Basic eligibility verification
- Customer grievance registration

**Stage 2: Credit Assessment (2-5 days)**
- Credit report generation from CIBIL/Equifax
- Income verification process
- Existing liabilities verification
- Personal/business background check
- Reference checks

**Stage 3: Verification & Valuation (3-7 days)**
- Site visit (if collateral involved)
- Property valuation (by approved valuers)
- Employer verification (for salaried)
- Business verification (for self-employed)
- Financial statement verification

**Stage 4: Underwriting & Committee Approval (2-5 days)**
- Risk assessment
- Loan committee review
- Final approval decision
- Loan offer generation

**Stage 5: Documentation (1-3 days)**
- Loan agreement execution
- Collateral documentation
- Insurance policy procurement
- NACH setup

**Stage 6: Disbursement (1-2 days)**
- Final verification of conditions
- Fund transfer authorization
- Actual funds disbursement

### Approval Timeline Summary

**Personal Loans:**
- Standard: 24-48 hours
- With collateral: 3-5 days
- Fast-track: 4-6 hours (pre-approved customers)
- Online: 15-20 minutes (pre-qualified)

**Business Loans:**
- Standard: 7-10 days
- With property valuation: 10-15 days
- Fast-track: 3-5 days (existing customers)
- Large loans (>₹1Cr): 15-30 days

### Disbursement Methods

**Direct Credit (Within 24 hours):**
- Funds transferred to customer's registered bank account
- Preferred method for online applications
- Immediate fund availability

**Check Disbursement:**
- Check issued in customer name
- Valid for 6 months from issue
- Can take 2-3 days to clear

**Partial Disbursement:**
- For project-based loans
- Staged release against milestones
- First tranche: 40-50% on sanction
- Subsequent tranches: On certification of completion

**Standing Instructions:**
- Regular EMI payments via NACH (Auto-debit)
- Also via internet banking, mobile app

### Loan Agreement Terms

**Key Terms Documented:**
- Principal amount, tenure, rate of interest
- EMI amount and due date
- Processing fee, documentation charges
- Insurance requirements
- Collateral details and margin
- Prepayment terms and charges
- Default clauses and remedies
- Guarantor liabilities
- Compliance obligations

**Insurance Requirements:**
- Personal loan: Credit life insurance (optional, recommended)
- Business loan: Property insurance (if collateral involved)
- Keyman insurance: For proprietor-dependent businesses

## Approval Decision Factors

### Positive Factors (Likely to Increase Approval Chance)
✓ Existing customer with good track record
✓ High credit score (750+)
✓ Low credit utilization (<30%)
✓ Consistent salary/business income
✓ Low debt-to-income ratio (<40%)
✓ Strong co-applicant/guarantor
✓ Collateral available (reduces risk)
✓ Government employee status
✓ Professional qualification (CA, doctor, engineer)
✓ Diversified income sources
✓ Growing business (for business loans)

### Negative Factors (May Reduce Approval Chance)
✗ Credit score <650
✗ Recent defaults or missed payments
✗ High credit utilization (>70%)
✗ Frequent job changes (<1 year tenure)
✗ High debt-to-income ratio (>50%)
✗ Inadequate income documentation
✗ Recent bankruptcy or restructuring
✗ Irregular or declining income
✗ No collateral available (high amounts)
✗ Declining business (for business loans)
✗ Recent litigation or legal cases

### Common Rejection Reasons

1. **Insufficient Income:**
   - Declared income insufficient for loan amount + existing obligations
   - Remedy: Apply for lower amount, add co-applicant

2. **Low Credit Score:**
   - Credit score below 650
   - Remedy: Improve score by paying existing dues, wait 3-6 months

3. **Default/Adverse Credit History:**
   - Recent defaults or judgments in credit report
   - Remedy: Wait for default to age (2-3 years), settle if in collections

4. **Incomplete Documentation:**
   - Missing or invalid documents
   - Remedy: Provide all required documents in proper format

5. **Loan Size Mismatch:**
   - Loan amount excessive for income/collateral
   - Remedy: Reduce loan amount or increase collateral/co-applicant income

6. **Negative Verification:**
   - Employer/business verification shows different information
   - Remedy: Provide authentic documentation, correct any discrepancies

7. **High Existing Liability:**
   - Total EMI >50% of monthly income (including proposed loan)
   - Remedy: Increase tenure, reduce loan amount, or clear existing debts

8. **Collateral Issues:**
   - Insufficient collateral value or title defects
   - Remedy: Provide additional collateral with clear titles

## Default & Recovery

### Definition & Consequences

**What Constitutes Default:**
- Missing EMI payment for 90+ days (3+ months)
- Missing EMI payment for 60+ days (2+ months) for secured loans
- Breach of loan terms and conditions
- Concealment of material facts

**Consequences of Default:**

1. **Credit Impact:**
   - Credit score reduction: 100-150 points per month of default
   - Credit report adverse mark for 7 years
   - Future credit becomes difficult and expensive

2. **Financial Penalties:**
   - Late payment interest: 2% per month (24% p.a.)
   - Penal interest on default: Additional 2-4% p.a.
   - Legal charges and recovery costs
   - Court charges (if legal action taken)

3. **Operational Actions:**
   - Account escalation to recovery team
   - Frequent reminder calls and letters
   - Debit facility restriction
   - Collateral seizure (if mortgaged)

4. **Legal Actions:**
   - Cheque bounce prosecution (under Negotiable Instruments Act)
   - Asset seizure and auction
   - Court judgment and recovery suit
   - Debt recovery suit in civil court
   - Debarment from banking services

### Recovery Process

**Stage 1: Preventive (Before Default)**
- Payment reminders: SMS/email 7 days before due date
- Pre-EMI communication: Proactive outreach
- Hardship programs: For financial difficulties
- Moratorium: Temporary payment pause (if eligible)

**Stage 2: Early Collections (30-90 Days)**
- Reminder letters and calls
- Increased contact frequency
- Field visit by loan officer
- Hardship assessment

**Stage 3: Active Recovery (90-180 Days)**
- Legal notice issued
- Account referred to recovery agency
- Credit bureau reporting (as delinquent)
- Garnishee proceedings initiated

**Stage 4: Legal Action (180+ Days)**
- Debt recovery suit in court
- Asset attachment and auction
- Garnishee order execution
- Cheque bounce case (if applicable)

### Restructuring & Workout Options

**Eligibility for Restructuring:**
- No willful default
- Default <90 days
- Some ability to pay
- First restructuring request

**Restructuring Options:**
1. **Tenure Extension:**
   - Extend repayment period (increases total interest)
   - Reduces monthly EMI burden

2. **Interest Concession:**
   - Waive penal interest
   - Reduce interest rate for remaining tenure
   - Payment holiday: Skip EMI for 1-2 months

3. **Principal Modification:**
   - Reduce principal (in rare cases)
   - Split into tranches with different repayment
   - Write-off (only in extreme hardship)

4. **Payment Plan:**
   - Catch-up plan for default amount
   - Resume regular EMI with catch-up portion

## Regulatory Compliance & Important Notes

### RBI Norms & Guidelines

**Mandatory Compliance:**
- All loans follow RBI Master Circular guidelines
- Customer Due Diligence (CDD) mandatory
- Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML) checks
- FEMA compliance for forex loans

**Fair Lending Practices:**
- No discrimination based on caste, religion, gender
- Transparent fee and rate structure
- Clear disclosure of all terms
- Grievance redressal mechanism
- Ombudsman access for disputes

### Important Disclaimers

⚠️ **Interest Rate Volatility:**
- Rates mentioned are as of April 2026
- Rates subject to change based on market conditions
- Final rate confirmed at time of sanction

⚠️ **Loan Approval Not Guaranteed:**
- Meeting eligibility criteria doesn't guarantee approval
- Final approval depends on comprehensive assessment
- Loan officer discretion applies

⚠️ **Income Documentation:**
- Self-certified income statements not acceptable
- Income must be verifiable from official sources
- Recent income documentation preferred

⚠️ **Collateral Liability:**
- Collateral may be seized in case of default
- Auction proceeds: Used to recover loan + costs
- Shortfall: Borrower liable for shortfall amount

⚠️ **Floating Rate Risk:**
- Floating rate can increase if base rate rises
- EMI may increase on rate reset
- Interest rate caps not applicable

### Post-Approval Obligations

**Borrower Responsibilities:**
- Timely payment of EMI
- Maintain insurance (if required)
- Keep collateral in good condition
- Inform bank of any changes (address, income, etc.)
- Maintain account in good standing
- Submit required documents/certificates

**Bank Rights:**
- Right to recover loan as per agreement
- Right to modify terms (with notice)
- Right to forfeit collateral
- Right to report to credit bureaus
- Right to pursue legal action for recovery
