## Account Opening

### Account Types

**Savings Account:**
- For personal savings and transactions
- Minimum opening balance: ₹0 (waived on first deposit)
- Monthly statement: Automatic
- Suitable for: Salaried, students, homemakers

**Current Account:**
- For business and commercial transactions
- Minimum opening balance: ₹10,000 - ₹25,000 (varies)
- Unlimited deposits and withdrawals
- Suitable for: Businesses, partnerships, trusts

**Recurring Deposit (RD):**
- Fixed amount deposited monthly for set tenure
- Interest: 3.5-5.5% p.a. (based on tenure)
- Minimum monthly deposit: ₹100-₹1,000
- Suitable for: Disciplined savings

**Salary Account:**
- Designed for salary credit
- Premium benefits with waived charges
- Zero minimum balance
- Free debit card
- Suitable for: Salaried employees

**Student Account:**
- Special account for students
- Zero minimum balance requirement
- Limited transaction limits
- No maintenance fees
- Suitable for: Full-time students

1. **Primary Identity Proof** (any one):

**For Business Entities:**
1. **Ownership Documents:**

2. **Authorization:**
   - Board resolution

3. **Personal Documents of Partners/Directors:**
   - Identity and address proof

## Account Operations & Transactions

### Transaction Limits

**ATM Withdrawals:**
- Limit: ₹50,000 per day (both own + other bank ATMs)
- Can be increased on request up to ₹1,00,000
- Minimum withdrawal: ₹100
- Maximum withdrawal: Single transaction ₹20,000

**Online Transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS):**
- IMPS: No limit (24x7 available)
- NEFT: No limit (processing 8 AM to 7 PM, next day credit)
- RTGS: Minimum ₹2 lakh only (for larger amounts)
- Daily limit: Can be customized via settings

**Cheque Transactions:**
- Cheque writing: No limit (subject to balance)
- Cheque clearance: 2-3 working days (local), 4-5 days (outstation)
- Stale cheque: Not valid after 6 months from issue date
- Cheque status: Track via mobile app/website

**Mobile/Internet Banking:**
- Fund transfer: No limit (secure with OTP)
- Bill payments: ₹10,000 per transaction (can be increased)
- Mobile recharge: ₹10,000 per transaction
- Utility payments: No limit (most utilities)

### Account Statements

**Statement Generation:**
- Monthly automatic e-statement: Email delivery
- Physical statement: On request (charges apply after 1 free per month)
- Statement delivery: 2-3 business days after month-end
- Statement access: Anytime via mobile app/website

**Statement Content:**
- Opening balance
- All transactions (date, type, amount, reference)
- Closing balance
- Interest credited
- Charges deducted
- Statement validation and bank details

**Statement Formats:**
- PDF download
- Email delivery
- Physical copy
- Alternate year statements (on request)

### Overdraft & Borrowing Against Account

**Overdraft Facility:**
- Available on savings account (₹5,000-₹50,000)
- Available on current account (up to agreed limit)
- Interest rate: 2-3% above savings rate (16-18% p.a.)
- Repayment: Flexible, on-demand basis

**Loan Against Account Balance:**
- Available on savings account
- Up to 90% of account balance
- Interest rate: FD rate + 2%
- Processing fee: ₹500-₹1,000
- Sanction time: 1-2 days

## Card Services & Policies

### Lost/Stolen Card Procedures

**Immediate Actions:**
1. **Report Immediately:**
   - Mobile app: 24x7 self-service blocking
   - Website: Click "Block Card" in 30 seconds
   - Call: 1-800-BANK-HELP (24-hour toll-free)
   - Branch: Visit nearest branch immediately
   - Email: Not recommended for urgent blocks

2. **Card Blocking:**
   - Automatic block within 5 minutes of report
   - SMS confirmation of block
   - No further transactions possible
   - Block is permanent (card cannot be unblocked)

3. **Replacement Card:**
   - Replacement card issued at no charge
   - Delivery: 5-7 business days (standard)
   - Expedited delivery: 24-48 hours (₹250 extra)
   - Temporary card: Available at nearest branch within 2-4 hours

**Liability & Protection:**
- ✓ Zero liability for unauthorized transactions if reported within 24 hours
- ✓ Liability limited to ₹0 (bank bears all losses)
- ✓ Provisional credit during investigation (max 30 days)
- ✓ Full refund if loss confirmed

### Duplicate Charges / Incorrect Debits

**Dispute Filing Process:**
1. **Report Within Timeline:**
   - Credit card: Within 90 days of statement date
   - Debit card: Within 60 days of transaction
   - Cash withdrawal: Immediate report recommended

2. **Information Required:**
   - Transaction date
   - Amount
   - Merchant/payee name
   - Transaction reference/confirmation number
   - Brief description of dispute

3. **Dispute Channels:**
   - Mobile app: "Report Transaction Issue"
   - Website: Dispute management portal
   - Email: support@bank.com with details
   - Call: Customer service with transaction details
   - Branch: In-person dispute filing with passbook

4. **Investigation Process:**
   - Acknowledgment within 24 hours (with reference number)
   - Initial investigation: 10 business days
   - Merchant contact & verification: 5-15 business days
   - Resolution report: Issued within 30 days

**Outcomes & Resolution:**
| Outcome | Timeline | Action |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Confirmed Error | 10-15 days | Full refund to original account |
| Disputed Amount | 15-30 days | Provisional credit released |
| Merchant Verified | 20-30 days | Dispute closed; no refund |
| Still Under Review | 30+ days | Extended investigation; periodic updates |

**Refund Process:**
- Refund credited to account used for transaction
- Credit confirmation via SMS/email
- Appears in statement next cycle
- No reversal charges or fees

### Card Replacement & Renewal

**Automatic Renewal:**
- Card automatically renewed 30 days before expiry
- New card mailed to registered address
- Existing card becomes inactive on expiry date
- Old card can be destroyed or returned

**Expedited Renewal:**
- Request online or via app
- Expedited fee: ₹250 for 2-day delivery

**Card Upgrade/Downgrade:**
- Upgrade to premium card: Online or branch request
- Downgrade to basic card: Any time without penalty
- New card number with upgrade
- Same card number with downgrade (if available)

### Fraud & Security

**24/7 Fraud Monitoring:**
- Real-time transaction monitoring
- Unusual pattern detection
- Immediate card block if suspicious activity
- OTP required for high-value transactions
- 3D Secure authentication for online purchases

**Security Best Practices:**

## Account Closure & Cessation

### Savings/Current Account Closure

**Why Close an Account:**
- Moving to another bank
- Consolidating accounts
- Inactivity / no requirement
- Relocation outside service area

**Closure Process:**

1. **Initiate Closure:**
   - Online request: Via mobile app/website (5 minutes)
   - Branch visit: In-person form submission
   - Email/Letter: Written request with signature

2. **Pre-Closure Checklist:**
   - ✓ Clear all outstanding cheques
   - ✓ Stop all recurring payments/EMIs
   - ✓ Cancel all standing instructions
   - ✓ Settle any loan/overdraft balance
   - ✓ Retrieve locker items (if applicable)

3. **Processing:**
   - Approval: 1-2 business days
   - Statement generation: Final statement issued
   - Balance refund: To registered account or cheque (3-5 days)
   - Confirmation: Email acknowledgment

**Charges for Closure:**
- Savings account: ₹0 (no charge)
- Current account: ₹0 (no charge)
- Locker: Return deposit (if paid)
- No penalty for premature closure

**After Closure:**
- Account marked as closed in records
- 7-year record retention (regulatory)
- Cheques of closed account returned unpaid
- No further transactions possible

### Fixed Deposit Closure / Premature Withdrawal

**Premature Withdrawal (Before Maturity):**

| Tenure Remaining | Interest Rate Applied | Penalty |
|------------------|----------------------|---------|
| <6 months | Savings rate (3.5%) | None |
| 6-12 months | Rate - 1% p.a. | 0.5% on interest |
| 12+ months | Rate - 0.5% p.a. | 0% penalty |

**Withdrawal Process:**
1. Request submission (online or branch)
2. Interest calculation as per above rules
3. Amount credited within 2-4 business days
4. Confirmation via SMS/email

**Loan Against FD (Better Option):**
- Borrow up to 90% of FD value
- FD remains intact & earns interest
- Loan interest: FD rate + 2%
- Repay anytime without penalty
- Better than premature withdrawal

### Credit Card Closure

**Closure Eligibility:**
- All outstanding balance paid
- No pending disputes
- No active loan against card
- Rewards redeemed or expired

**Closure Steps:**

1. **Pre-Closure:**
   - Clear outstanding balance completely
   - No pending transactions
   - Redeem rewards points (if any)
   - Submit written request or request online

2. **Processing:**
   - Verification of zero balance: 1 day
   - Final statement issued: 2-3 days
   - Card cancellation confirmation: 5 days

3. **Physical Card:**
   - Cut card into pieces or return to branch
   - Confirmation letter issued
   - Reference number provided

**Post-Closure Impact:**
- Credit limit becomes zero
- Credit history retained (positive impact)
- Card not usable after closure
- Future reactivation not possible

## Return Policy & Refunds

### Refund Eligibility

**Eligible for Refund:**
✓ Duplicate charges (charged twice for one transaction)
✓ Unauthorized transactions (disputed within 60-90 days)
✓ Merchant errors (incorrect amount charged)
✓ Service cancellation refunds (subscription cancelled)
✓ Returned/rejected goods refunds
✓ Billing disputes (discrepancy in amount)

**Not Eligible for Refund:**
✗ Voluntary purchases (changed mind, regret)
✗ Subscription already used (e.g., movie watched)
✗ Gift card/store credits (merchant terms apply)
✗ Expired promotional vouchers
✗ Purchases from unauthorized sellers (marketplace discretion)

### Refund Timeline

**By Refund Type:**

| Refund Type | Investigation Time | Refund Processing | Total Timeline |
|------------|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Duplicate Charge | 3-5 days | 5-7 days | 10-12 days |
| Merchant Error | 5-7 days | 5-7 days | 10-14 days |
| Unauthorized (Card) | 10-15 days | 5-7 days | 15-22 days |
| Unauthorized (UPI) | 5-10 days | Immediate | 5-10 days |
| Subscription Refund | 1-2 days | 2-3 days | 3-5 days |
| Marketplace Refund | 5-10 days | 5-10 days | 10-20 days |
| International | 10-20 days | 10-20 days | 20-40 days |

**Refund Status Tracking:**
- Real-time status: Mobile app/website portal
- Reference number provided at filing
- Email updates: Filed, Under Review, Resolved
- SMS notification upon refund processing
- Statement reflection: Next billing cycle

### Refund Processing & Payment Method

**Refund Credit Options:**
1. **Original Payment Method (Preferred):**
   - Refunded to same card/account used
   - Credit within stated timeline
   - No intermediate steps required
   - Preferred for customer convenience

2. **Bank Transfer (NEFT):**
   - If original method unavailable
   - To registered bank account
   - Processing: 1-2 business days
   - Confirmation via SMS

3. **Check (Rare):**
   - Issued only if other methods unavailable
   - Delivery: 5-7 business days
   - Clearing time: 2-3 additional days
   - Payee name same as account holder

**Refund Reversal (Chargeback):**
- Refund becomes final once processed
- Cannot be reversed by customer
- Bank can reverse if fraud detected
- Legal hold: If legal proceedings ongoing

### Refund Amount & Calculation

**Full Refund:**
- 100% of disputed transaction amount
- Plus interest (if applicable, on extended delays >30 days)
- Minus documented losses (if confirmed fraud by customer)

**Partial Refund:**
- If partial use/service confirmed
- Merchant calculates return amount
- Bank processes approved amount
- Customer notified of calculation details

**Interest on Delayed Refund:**
- Applies if refund delayed >30 days beyond investigation
- Interest rate: 10% p.a. on refunded amount
- Calculated from original transaction date
- Applied automatically by bank

## FAQs - Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I maintain multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, you can have multiple savings accounts, but only one primary account for salary credit. Inform bank about all accounts.

**Q: What happens if I don't maintain minimum balance?**
A: For accounts requiring minimum balance, ₹50/month penalty applies if balance falls short. Waived for first 6 months.

**Q: Can I change account type?**
A: Yes, you can convert savings to current (with letter request) or vice versa, processing within 5 business days.

**Q: How do I update my nominee?**
A: Online banking > Account > Update Nominee, or branch form. Requires signature and self-attested ID proof copy.

**Q: Can I issue cheques after account closure?**
A: No, cheques from closed accounts are returned unpaid. Remaining cheques should be destroyed.

**Q: How long does NEFT take?**
A: NEFT transactions processed in batches (every hour, 8 AM to 7 PM). Money reaches within 24 hours, usually 2-4 hours.

**Q: Is there a limit on cheque amount?**
A: No limit on cheque amount, but it must not exceed account balance. High-value cheques may be specially handled.

**Q: What is standing instruction?**
A: Recurring automatic payment setup for EMIs, bills, subscriptions. Can be modified or cancelled anytime with 30-day notice.

**Q: Can I get cash against a post-dated cheque (PDC)?**
A: Only if cheque is made payable in advance. Need to provide cheque with post-dating details. Not available for all accounts.

**Q: What if my card is declined despite sufficient balance?**
A: Multiple possible reasons: daily limit exceeded, suspicious activity flag, technical issue. Contact support for immediate resolution.

**Q: How do I authorize someone to operate my account?**
A: Power of Attorney (POA) can be granted at branch with legal documentation. Limited authority possible via joint account.

**Q: Can I get a duplicate FD certificate?**
A: Yes, request online (2-3 days) or at branch (same day). Nominal charge: ₹50. Required for loan against FD.

**Q: What are the tax implications of FD interest?**
A: FD interest is taxable as income. TDS (20%) deducted if interest >₹10,000. Senior citizens get lower exemption (₹50,000).

**Q: Is there a fee for balance inquiry?**
A: No, balance inquiries are free via ATM, app, website, phone banking. Monthly statement also free (physical charges apply).

**Q: What happens to unclaimed accounts?**
A: Accounts inactive for 2+ years marked as dormant. Reactivation possible at branch with ID verification. After 10 years, transferred to RBI.
